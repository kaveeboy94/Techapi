Colombo University

  • Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology

    This degree offers the following career pathways:

    • Software Developer: Design, develop, and maintain software applications for various platforms.
    • IT Consultant: Provide expert advice to businesses on technology-related matters.
    • Cloud Solutions Architect: Design and implement cloud-based solutions for businesses.
    • UX/UI Designer: Create intuitive and visually appealing interfaces for software applications and websites.
    • Data Analyst: Collect, process, and analyze data to provide insights for business decisions.
    • IT Project Manager: Oversee the planning, execution, and successful delivery of technology projects.
    • Mobile App Developer: Specialize in creating applications for smartphones and other mobile devices.
    • IT Support Specialist: Provide technical assistance and troubleshooting to end-users.

    Degree Information:

    Information and Communication Technology

  • Bachelor of Engineering Technology

    This degree offers the following career pathways:

    • Automation Engineer: Design, develop, and implement automated systems.
    • Instrumentation Engineer: Specialize in designing and maintaining instruments and sensors.
    • Control Systems Engineer: Work on developing and optimizing control systems in various industries.
    • Process Automation Specialist: Focus on automating and optimizing manufacturing and industrial processes.
    • Manufacturing Automation Technician: Involved in the setup, maintenance, and troubleshooting of automated manufacturing systems.
    • Instrumentation and Control Specialist: Focus on specific aspects of instrumentation and control systems.
    • Maintenance Engineer (Automation): Responsible for ensuring the proper functioning of automated systems.
    • Energy Management Engineer: Optimize energy usage in buildings and industrial facilities.
    • Calibration Engineer: Calibrate and adjust instruments and sensors for accurate measurements.
    • Teacher: Can teach technology subjects in government or private schools.

    Degree Information:

    Instrumentation and Automation Technology

  • Bachelor of Biosystem Technology

    Degree Information and Career Pathways:

    Environmental Technology Agriculture Technology
    • Environmental Consultant: Assess environmental impacts and develop strategies to minimize negative effects.
    • Environmental Scientist: Conduct research and investigations to understand environmental issues and propose solutions.
    • Sustainability Analyst: Evaluate organizational practices for environmental sustainability.
    • Waste Management Specialist: Focus on the proper handling, treatment, and disposal of waste materials.
    • Renewable Energy Consultant: Advise on the adoption of renewable energy sources.
    • Environmental Policy Analyst: Research and assess environmental policies and regulations.
    • Natural Resource Manager: Oversee sustainable use and conservation of natural resources.
    • Environmental Health and Safety Specialist: Ensure compliance with environmental and safety regulations.
    • Ecologist: Study ecosystems and their interactions with living organisms.
    • Water Quality Specialist: Assess and monitor water quality.
    • Agricultural Research Scientist: Conduct experiments to improve crop yield and quality.
    • Agronomist: Focus on crop management and production, advising farmers on best practices.
    • Precision Agriculture Specialist: Use advanced technologies to optimize farming practices.
    • Agricultural Consultant: Provide expert advice to farmers and agricultural businesses.
    • Farm Manager: Oversee daily farm operations.
    • Sustainable Agriculture Coordinator: Promote environmentally friendly farming practices.
